The Hero of the Shift

Being an All-Rounder is more than just a job title. It means you are the glue that holds the shift together. When things get busy or challenges pop up, you are the one the team looks to. You won't just be doing a job; you'll be learning the entire Jumbo operation from the inside out and keeping everything running smoothly.

Your Impact

How you'll make an impact every day

Five core responsibilities. One versatile role. You'll move between them seamlessly and keep the whole operation flowing.

01
Help Desk
Put on that famous pink vest and be our go-to expert. Using your laptop, you'll dive into the Jumbo system to fix issues and help your colleagues succeed. You're the one who keeps everyone moving.
02
Reach Truck Driver
Jump in the driver's seat and keep the warehouse flowing. You'll make sure pallets are perfectly placed and refilled in both the KW section and the fresh AGF (fruits and vegetables) area.
03
Order Control
Equip the team with working scanners and keep a sharp eye on our orders, guaranteeing everything leaving our warehouse is top quality and ready to go.
04
Ontvangst
Take charge at the docks. You'll be the first to welcome incoming trucks and dive right into the fast-paced receiving and filling process to keep the stock high.
05
HHD / Housekeeper
A great shift starts with a great environment. You'll lead the way in making sure our department is clean, safe, and an awesome place to work.
